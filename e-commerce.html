1 Program Overview

Our Bachelor of E-commerce is a three-and-a-half-year undergraduate program, specifically designed to prepare students for the contemporary workforce and a global career, especially for the emerging market. The program aims to equip students with effective digital knowledge and a strong commercial grounding in business by combining theory and practice to build their understanding of business operations alongside technology development. Students will not only gain analytical, technical, and practical skills, but also soft skills that are integrated into the curriculum in order to provide students with critical thinking, problem-solving, leadership skills and a global mindset so that they can apply their knowledge effectively in the future career.

3 Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this program the students will be able to:

    •   Identify problems and formulate business solutions using technology tools
    •   Identify the consumer’s behaviors and needs
    •   Recognize the innovative use of E-Commerce to develop a competitive advantage
    •   Identify key problems hindering successful communication
    •   Communicate with customers effectively and ethically using electronic media
    •   Develop technology tools and content strategies essential for running a business
    •   Analyze branding and pricing strategies
    •   Apply effective digital communication strategies and tools to boost business
