Established since 2013 by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of Cambodia (MPTC), National Institute of Posts, Telecoms and Information Communication Technology (NIPTICT) is a public research and training institution.

Three Things That Make NIPTICT Stand Out:

  • NIPTICT Specializes in ICT Research and Training: Established under the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of Cambodia.
  • First Telecommunications and E-Commerce: NIPTICT is the first telecommunications and e-commerce school in Cambodia.
  • The Three Components: Training program at NIPTICT is composed of three components: 1) Technical Skill, 2) Soft Skill, and 3) Entrepreneurship.
